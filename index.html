I'm a scientist at Sapience Therapeutics and I work on molecular design.

~A winding path~

I grew up on a small island (Ambergris Caye) in what was a fishing village (San Pedro Town) of around 1000 people in Belize (Central America). I was born to conservationist parents who moved to Belize to start a field research station in the early 90s (Belize Tropical Research and Education Center (TREC)) that hosted countless universities, university professors, and their respective research programs.

Growing up in the presence of one of the most spectacular ecosystems on Earth (Mesoamerican Barrier Reef System) gave me the opportunity to observe the curiousities of Nature, and long discussions with my parents and the various academics we hosted taught me to appreciate it (though, I'm still working to understand it!)
